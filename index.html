About Adam

Adam Walker is a young lad from Edmonton Canada who enjoys spending his time reading, training, working as a personal-caregiver. Adam also builds physics engines on the side. At the moment he just wants some Fide points.

Community Engagement

Giving back to his community is a way that Adam enjoys spending his time. He has volunteered in a wide array of positions from coaching special Olympics to local thrift shops and camp counseling.

Ski Racing

After receiving 5 concussion in 7 months Rugby was looking less and less like the sport to pursue. Cross country skiing was something Adam had dabbled in to maintain fitness during the winters. In his first year racing Adam competed at the national level and is excited to see where skiing takes him.
