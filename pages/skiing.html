Ski Racing

After several consecutive concussions in rugby I needed to switch sports. Cross country skiing was a natural pivot as I used it to train my fitness for rugby already. Having skied a little bit I figured I was a pretty decent skier. My coach who loves nothing more than watching Norwegian technique videos showed me I had a long way to go. The 2022-23 season is my second season racing and I am traveling the country, competing at the highest level in Canada.

I have enjoyed my training immensely and will continue to train in order to achieve my goals for the 2023-2024 season.

Home Club: Edmonton Nordic
Favorite Training Site: Victoria Oval(West loop)
Best Race of 2023: Canadian Birkebeiner-1st Place (31km)
Sponsors: KVPlus, Track 'N Trail
