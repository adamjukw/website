About Adam

Adam Walker is a young man from Edmonton, Canada. He enjoys spending his time reading, training, working as a personal-caregiver and playing chess. Adam also builds physics engines on the side.

Community Engagement

Giving back to his community is essential to Adam. He has volunteered in a wide array of positions from coaching special Olympics to local thrift shops and camp counseling as well as many other positions.

Ski Racing

Five concussions in seven months put a halt to Adam's Rugby career. Cross country skiing was something Adam had used in to maintain fitness during the winters and was a natural transition. In his first year racing Adam competed at the national level and is excited to see where skiing takes him in the coming year.
