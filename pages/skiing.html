Ski Racing

After several consecutive concussions in rugby I needed to switch sports. Cross country skiing was a natural pivot as I used it to train my fitness for rugby already. The 2022-23 season is only my second season and I am traveling the country, racing at the highest level in Canada.

I have enjoyed training immensely and plan to continue racing in the 2023-2024 season.
